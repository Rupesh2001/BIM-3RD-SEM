Welcome to KCMIT

The Kantipur College of Management and Information Technology (KCMIT) has been set up in Kathmandu under Tribhuvan University new direction to enter into collaboration with the private education sector to provide the most advanced technical education to the Nepalese students. Presently, KCMIT runs the Bachelor of Information Management (BIM) program and Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program in affiliation with Tribhuvan University. BIM is a blend of management and IT and BBA is a modern management course with IT application.

Faculty

BIM

Bachelor in Information Management

The BIM program is designed to equip graduates with the skills and attributes required to be effective and efficient information technology professionlas.

BBA

Bachelor of Business Administration

BBA is a four years program designed to develop socially responsive, creative, and result oriented management professionlas.

BCA

Bachelor in Computer Application

BCA is an Information Technology based career that awards the undergraduate degree in Computer Applications. This program provides a sound academic base for an advanced career in computer application.
